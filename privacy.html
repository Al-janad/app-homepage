Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 03, 2025

Thank you for using مهمتي (My Task). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Core Privacy Principle: "مهمتي" is designed as a Local-First application. This means your Tasks, Habits, Plans, and Notes are stored directly on your device's internal storage. We do not transmit this personal data to our own servers.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data (Local Storage)

We do not collect or store your personal tasks, habits, or schedules on external servers. All data you enter into the app remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up to your personal Google Drive.

B. Permissions and Device Access

To provide specific features, we request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To store your tasks and settings locally.
  • Internet Access: To facilitate Google Sign-In, Google Drive backups, Email Scheduling, and serve advertisements.
  • Alarm/Notifications: To send you reminders for your tasks and habits.

2. Google Services & User Data Policy

Our app integrates with Google Services to provide "Email Scheduling" and "Cloud Backup".

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

مهمتي (My Task) use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We strictly use the restricted scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send for the sole purpose of:

  • Sending emails that you explicitly compose and schedule within the app.

We do NOT:

  • Read, view, or access your incoming emails (Gmail Read-only access is NOT requested).
  • Share your email content with third parties or advertisers.
  • Store your email content on our servers.

Google Drive (Backup)

If you use the backup feature, we access your Google Drive configuration solely to upload or download the specific data file generated by our app. We cannot access other files in your Drive.

3. Third-Party Advertising

We may use third-party advertising companies (specifically Google AdMob) to serve ads when you visit the Application. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites contained in web cookies and tracking technologies to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

You can manage your ad preferences via your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID).

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. Since authentication handles are managed via OAuth 2.0 directly with Google, we never have access to your passwords.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" of this policy. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
